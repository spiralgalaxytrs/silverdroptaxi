Account Deletion Policy

Effective date: 2025-09-23

1. Introduction

Welcome to Silver Drop Taxi.

Overview

This policy outlines the process for deleting your account on Silvertaxi. You can delete your account through our app by accessing the account settings in your profile.

How to Delete Your Account

Via App:

1. Open the Silvertaxi app and log in to your account.

2. Navigate to the profile or account settings section.

3. Select the "Delete Account" option.

4. Follow the prompts to confirm the deletion.

Via Website:

1. Log in to your account on the Silvertaxi website.

2. Go to your profile settings.

3. Click on the "Delete Account" link.

4. Confirm your decision by following the instructions provided.

What Happens After Deletion

Upon successful deletion of your account: - Your account and all associated data will be permanently removed from our active databases. - Some information may be retained in our backup archives to comply with legal requirements, prevent fraud, troubleshoot issues, or enforce our terms of service.

Effects of Deletion

Loss of Access:You will no longer have access to your account or any associated services and data.

Irreversible Action: Account deletion is irreversible. Once completed, you will not be able to recover any information or data previously associated with your account.

Questions or Assistance

If you have any questions or need assistance with account deletion, please contact our support team at tel: 9786999777.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email: silveronewaytaxi@gmail.com.
